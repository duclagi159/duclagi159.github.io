Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tool Beautiful may collect, use, store, and protect information when a user connects an authorized TikTok account or uses the application.

1. Information we may process

Depending on the permissions granted by the user, Tool Beautiful may process the following information:

  • TikTok display name and username.
  • Profile image and publicly available profile information.
  • Follower, following, likes, and video-count statistics.
  • Information about public videos belonging to the authorized account.
  • Video view, like, comment, and share statistics when available.
  • Authorization status and token-related information needed to maintain the connection.
  • Locally configured account labels, notes, groups, and application settings.

2. How information is obtained

TikTok account information is requested only after the account owner completes TikTok's official authorization process and grants the requested permissions.

Tool Beautiful does not require users to provide their TikTok password directly to the application.

3. How information is used

Information may be used to:

  • Display the authorized account's profile information.
  • Display account and public-video statistics.
  • Connect an authorized TikTok account to a local browser profile.
  • Provide account-management and content-workflow features.
  • Maintain the user's selected settings and application state.
  • Detect connection errors and refresh authorized access when permitted.

4. Data storage

Tool Beautiful is a desktop application. Application settings, account mappings, statistics snapshots, and authorization information may be stored locally on the user's computer.

Users are responsible for protecting access to their computer and operating-system account.

5. Data sharing and selling

Tool Beautiful does not sell TikTok account information or personal information to advertisers or unrelated third parties.

Information may be transmitted to TikTok or another service only when necessary to perform a feature requested by the user or when required by law.

6. Data retention

Locally stored information may remain on the user's computer until the user disconnects an account, deletes the application data, or uninstalls the application.

Authorization credentials are retained only for as long as needed to maintain the connection or until the user revokes access.

7. Revoking access

Users may revoke Tool Beautiful's access through their TikTok account settings or use the disconnect function provided by the application when available.

Revoking authorization prevents future authorized API requests, but locally stored information may still need to be deleted separately from the user's computer.

8. Deleting locally stored data

Users may remove locally stored Tool Beautiful settings and account information by using the application's deletion or disconnect controls, or by deleting the application's local data from their computer.

Users may contact the address below for assistance with a data deletion request.

9. Security

Reasonable technical measures are used to protect stored authorization information and application data. However, no storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

10. Children's privacy

Tool Beautiful is not intended for children under the minimum age required to use TikTok or other connected services in their country. The application does not knowingly collect information from children in violation of applicable law.

11. Third-party services

TikTok and other connected services operate under their own privacy policies and terms. Tool Beautiful is not responsible for the privacy practices, availability, or security of third-party services.

12. Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when application features, permissions, storage practices, or legal requirements change.
