Privacy Policy

Effective and last updated: July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Tool Beautiful processes and protects information when a user uses the Windows desktop application or connects a TikTok account through TikTok's official authorization flow.

1. Service and data controller

Tool Beautiful is an independently developed Windows desktop application. It provides local video utilities and a Manager Account dashboard for TikTok accounts explicitly authorized by the account owner. Support and privacy requests are handled through the contact methods on the Support page.

2. Information processed

Depending on the permissions granted by the user, Tool Beautiful may process TikTok display name, username, profile image, profile details, follower and following counts, total likes, video count, public-video metadata, and available view, like, comment, and share statistics.

The application also processes authorization status, access and refresh credentials needed to maintain the connection, locally configured settings, synchronization timestamps, and technical error information needed to operate the feature.

3. How information is obtained

TikTok information is obtained only after the account owner completes TikTok Login Kit authorization and grants the requested permissions. Tool Beautiful does not ask users to enter their TikTok password into the application.

Information may also be provided directly by the user through local application settings or generated locally when the application records synchronization status and errors.

4. Purposes of processing

  • Authenticate and identify an authorized TikTok account.
  • Display profile information and account statistics.
  • List the authorized account's public videos and available performance metrics.
  • Refresh authorized access when TikTok permits it.
  • Maintain user-selected settings and diagnose connection errors.
  • Provide disconnect, revoke, and local-data deletion controls.

5. Permissions requested

The TikTok integration may request user.info.basic, user.info.profile, user.info.stats, and video.list. Each permission is used only for the corresponding profile, statistics, or public-video feature described on the official website.

The current integration does not use TikTok permissions to publish, edit, or delete content.

6. Local storage and security

Tool Beautiful is a desktop application. Account mappings, settings, statistics snapshots, and authorization credentials are stored locally on the user's computer. Sensitive TikTok credentials are protected with Windows Data Protection API mechanisms tied to the user's Windows account.

7. Sharing and sale of data

Tool Beautiful does not sell TikTok account information or personal information to advertisers or unrelated third parties. Data is transmitted to TikTok when required to authenticate, refresh authorization, retrieve requested information, revoke access, or comply with law.

The application does not operate an advertising data marketplace and does not use TikTok data for cross-service behavioral advertising.

8. Retention

Locally stored information remains until the user disconnects the account, removes the application's local data, or uninstalls the application. Authorization credentials are retained only while needed to maintain the connection or until access is revoked.

TikTok may retain information under its own policies independently of Tool Beautiful.

9. User choices, access revocation, and deletion

Users can stop future API access by selecting Disconnect in Manager Account or revoking access in their TikTok account settings. Detailed local-data removal steps are provided on the Data Deletion Instructions page.

10. Children's privacy

Tool Beautiful is not directed to children below the minimum age required to use TikTok in their country and does not knowingly process children's information in violation of applicable law.

11. Third-party services and international processing

TikTok operates under its own Privacy Policy and Terms. Requests to TikTok may be processed in locations determined by TikTok. Tool Beautiful is not responsible for TikTok's independent data practices.

12. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when application functionality, permissions, storage practices, or legal requirements change. The effective date above will be revised when material changes are published.

13. Contact and data requests

Privacy questions, revocation assistance, and local-data deletion requests can be submitted through the official Tool Beautiful Support page or the developer's public GitHub contact profile.
